United States

The USA will host matches across major cities including Los Angeles, New York/New Jersey, Dallas, Miami, Seattle, Atlanta, Philadelphia, Boston, Houston, Kansas City, and San Francisco Bay Area.

Canada

Canada hosts matches in Toronto and Vancouver, giving fans a chance to experience modern cities, multicultural food, and passionate football crowds.

Mexico

Mexico hosts matches in Mexico City, Guadalajara, and Monterrey. The opening match takes place at Estadio Azteca in Mexico City.
