Tickets

How to Purchase Tickets Safely

1. Use FIFA only

Go directly to FIFA.com/tickets. Avoid random social media sellers or suspicious links.

2. Create FIFA ID

Sign in or create your FIFA ID before buying. Ticket access usually requires an official account.

3. Check availability

Use official sales phases, last-minute sales, or FIFA’s official marketplace when available.

Always verify ticket information from FIFA’s official ticket page before purchasing.
