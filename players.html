Stand-out Players

Big Names & Rising Stars

Cristiano Ronaldo

Portugal captain, veteran and one of the greatest Forwards.

Often referred to as one of the greatest to ever play the game, he is the all-time leading goalscorer in men's international football and has claimed five Ballon d'Or awards.

Lionel Messi

Argentina captain and 2022 World Cup winner.

Known for his unmatched vision, dribbling, and record eight Ballon d'Or awards. He is the all-time top scorer for both FC Barcelona and the Argentina national team.

Kylian Mbappé

France forward known for speed, finishing, and major tournament impact.

Mbappé is a dynamic and explosive forward. His blistering speed, technical dribbling, and composure in high-pressure situations make him one of the most feared attackers in world football

Lamine Yamal

Spain winger and one of the most exciting young attackers in world football.

Erling Haaland

Norway vice-captain, One of Europe's prolific Striker.

Haaland is a true goal-scoring phenomenon. Combining blistering pace, incredible strength, and clinical finishing

Harry Kane

England frontman has established himself as an elite, record-breaking finisher and a top Ballon d'Or contender.
